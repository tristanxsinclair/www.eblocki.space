# Eblocki — Product Spec

## One-liner
Stop fake productivity. Log proof. Get the next command. Compound real identity evidence.

## Category
Proof-based performance app / behavioural evidence operating system for students, operators, and builders.

## Ideal customer profile (ICP)
- Ambitious student, early-career operator, or founder building compound proof over 6–24 months.
- Frustrated with journalling, streaks, and productivity apps that reward activity over outcomes.
- Wants a system that judges their work honestly and tells them the next best move.

## Value proposition
1. Submit one measurable proof artifact per day.
2. Receive a verdict (weak / useful / strong / elite) with reasoning.
3. Receive one next best command.
4. Compound identity evidence over weeks and months.

## Differentiators
- Deterministic proof scoring, not subjective "wins".
- Court of Evidence: adversarial review of your own work.
- Identity Ledger: append-only evidence log.
- No fake success states, no hype gamification.

## Core loop
check-in → proof artifact → proof scoring → state detection → intervention → Court of Evidence → adversarial review → identity ledger → weekly review → Sentinel risk forecast.
